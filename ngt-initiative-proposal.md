## 2. Mission

> **To make every person who touches an NGT product more capable than they were before — not because of NGT, but because of themselves.**

NGT does not hand people answers. It hands people better questions, better frameworks, and better systems — and then gets out of the way.

---

## 3. Vision

> **To become the standard for how Indonesia — and eventually the world — produces its next generation of thinkers, builders, and pioneers.**

The long-term north star is a national Learning Management System that doesn't just track attendance and grades, but actively develops the cognitive capacity of every student inside it. This is a decade-long project. It begins now, one product and one student at a time.

---

## 4. The Problem We Exist to Solve

The Indonesian education system has two critical failures:

**Failure 1 — Content Without Capability**
Students learn *what* to think. They are rarely taught *how* to think. They can memorize, but they struggle to create, critique, or build from scratch.

**Failure 2 — Tools That Do the Work For You**
The rise of AI has created a generation of students who delegate thinking to tools. Most edtech platforms make tasks easier — not students better. This accelerates the problem.

**NGT's answer to both:** Products that serve the student's growth, not their convenience. Every NGT tool is designed to make the user work — better, not less.

---

## 5. Brand Philosophy

### *You are the next big thing.*

This is not a tagline. It is a design constraint. Every decision NGT makes — product, feature, content, community — is filtered through one question:

> *Does this make the user more capable, or does it make them more dependent?*

If it creates dependency, it doesn't ship.

## 6. The Product Pipeline

NGT operates as a holding company. Each product has its own branding, its own audience, and its own purpose — but they form a deliberate pipeline.

```
THINK BETTER → WRITE BETTER → LEARN BETTER → BUILD BETTER
```

| Product | Stage | Audience | Core Purpose |
|---------|-------|----------|--------------|
| **The Nexus** | Live | High schoolers (SMA) | A Notion-based system to organize thinking, tasks, and life — building the habit of structured thought. |
| **Project Arkais** | In Development | College students | An AI-assisted research and writing platform that reduces AI-generated noise by 99% — so the work stays the student's. |
| **NGT LMS** *(codename)* | Concept | All students, schools, institutions | A national-scale Learning Management System with an AI study partner at its core — one that interrogates, scaffolds, and challenges rather than generates. |

### The AI Study Partner Principle *(core to the LMS)*

The AI inside NGT products is trained with a fundamental constraint:

> **It must never reduce the student's cognitive load — only redirect it.**

- It does not write essays. It asks: *"What is your argument?"*
- It does not solve problems. It asks: *"What have you tried?"*
- It does not generate answers. It reflects: *"Walk me through your thinking."*

This is the most differentiated bet NGT is making. An AI that deliberately does less — but produces students who can do more.

## 9. Sequencing Strategy

NGT will not build everything at once. The order of operations is deliberate:

```
Phase 1 (Now)         → The Nexus live. Build community. Validate the "think better" layer.
Phase 2 (6–12 months) → Ship Project Arkais. College students become the R&D lab
                         for the AI study partner behavior.
Phase 3 (12–24 months)→ Port the study partner AI into the LMS. Start with one school
                         or institution. Prove the model small before scaling.
Phase 4 (3–5 years)   → Expand the LMS. Build toward national standard — slowly,
                         with community feedback, one institution at a time.
```

The LMS is the destination. Arkais is the proof of concept. The Nexus is the seed.
